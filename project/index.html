Grade : XI

New Horizon Higher Secondary School, located in Tansen-7, Kajipauwa, Palpa, is a well-established private educational institution that has been serving the community since its founding in 1992 AD (2049 BS). Affiliated with Nepal’s National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education, the school offers programs from pre-primary to Grade 10, as well as Plus Two (Class 11 and 12) in Science and Management streams. Known for its commitment to academic excellence, it provides quality education at a moderate fee structure and also supports deserving students through scholarship schemes. Over the decades, New Horizon has built a reputation for strong infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and a vibrant learning environment, making it one of the leading schools in Palpa for nurturing both academic and extracurricular talents.
